Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Refined by Passion

"Every dish we serve carries the memory of a grandmother's kitchen and the vision of tomorrow."

Zafran was born from a simple belief: that Pakistani cuisine deserves a stage worthy of its depth and complexity. Our founder, Chef Nasreen Malik, grew up surrounded by the aromas of slow-cooked daals, fragrant biryanis, and intricately spiced curries — flavors she spent two decades refining at culinary institutes across Europe and Asia.

Today, our kitchen blends heirloom recipes with modern technique — sourcing whole spices from the markets of Lahore's Anarkali bazaar and using contemporary plating to present them in ways that feel both timeless and entirely new.
