Our Philosophy

Cuisine as an act of care.

At Maison Élise, we believe that fine dining is not about ceremony — it is about connection. Every element on the plate, from the hand-foraged herbs to the wines selected by our sommelier, is chosen to create a single cohesive experience that lingers long after the evening ends.

Our Story

Born in a market kitchen.
Perfected over decades.

"I cook the way my grandmother taught me — with patience, with love, and with complete disregard for shortcuts."

Chef Élise Laurent opened Maison Élise in 1998 in the 7th arrondissement of Paris with eleven seats and a single tasting menu. What began as a quiet labour of love became one of the most talked-about openings in the French capital that decade.

Today, with three Michelin stars and a team of forty, the ethos has not shifted: every dish is made from scratch, every ingredient is sourced personally, and every guest receives the same unwavering attention.
